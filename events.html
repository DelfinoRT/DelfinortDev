What is the Red Rose?


We are a group/clan of players of Tibia MMORPG, one of the oldest and most successful massively multiplayer online role-playing games (MMORPG) created in Europe. In an MMORPG, people from all over the world meet on a virtual playground to explore areas, solve tricky riddles and undertake heroic quests.

Red Rose is an ancient guild of Good, followers of the God Banor. We are peaceloving and try to uphold law and noble virtues, but like the rose we have sharp thorns, too! - Founded December 7th 1998 - Proud winner of the first ever Tibian Football League.

What about the Rose Shields?

The history of the rose shields starts not long after Guido joined the CIPsoft team, the Gods decided to bless the oldest guilds of Tibia. Red Rose together with only other two guilds (the Mercenarys and Kaerell) were offered to design their own armor pieces. While the Red Rose designed the Rose shield and the Rose armor, the Mercenarys and Kaerell just designed shields.

The complete history of these shields and other interesting histories related to the Red Rose can be found on the history section of the website.
