Welcome!

We are an ancient guild of Good, followers of Banor.

We are peaceloving and try to uphold law and noble virtues, but like the rose we have sharp thorns, too!

Founded December 7th 1998

Proud winner of the first ever Tibian Football League - Recruitment is currently open!

Our home on Antica is Guildhall of the Red Rose in the Fibula island

Read More About Us

Goals of the Red Rose:

» Protection of its members


» Spreading and strengthening of the good alignment in the name of Banor


» Protection of all dogs in Tibia, because they are messengers of the gods

» Offering assistance to other Tibians


» Hosting events, quests, and guided tours


» Cooperation with Tibia-related forums

» Building friendships between members


» Building and strengthening bonds between the Red Rose and friendly or allied guild
